About Me

This project was created by Stanley Juarez, a student at Bronx Community College, for my BIS 23 class.

🎬 Why Horror?

Horror has always been my favorite genre. There's nothing quite like the thrill of a good scare — the suspense, the atmosphere, the feeling that anything could happen. Whether it's a slow-burn supernatural story or a full-on slasher, I'm always down to watch it. Horror movies have a way of bringing people together, and I've always been the friend in the group with a recommendation ready.

🌐 Why I Built This Site

Night Screams is currently a project for my BIS 23 class at Bronx Community College. I love horror movies, so I decided to build a site centered around them. The goal is to share horror films, collect reviews, and help other horror fans discover movies they might enjoy.
